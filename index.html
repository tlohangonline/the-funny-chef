The Funnychef's Delicious Dishes is Lebogang Tlokana's debut cookbook. In this book, she is taking it to the basics with recipes that will not only feed your cravings but turn you into a great cook in your own kitchen.

Let the Funny Chef show you the basics with her most sought-after featured recipes for: stocks to flavor dishes, soups for all seasons to best use your trimmings and leftovers creatively, families' favorite dishes, African cuisine and many more exciting recipes.
